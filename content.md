## Case Study 1

- **Client name:** Sourceflow
- **Industry:** B2B Software / Talent Acquisition
- **What they do:** Sourceflow builds talent acquisition software that helps recruitment agencies run faster, smarter hiring workflows.
- **What we built:** A custom-branded landing page integrating Sourceflow's job portal software for one of their recruitment clients.

**The problem they had before us:**
Their client needed a professional web presence built around Sourceflow's platform — fast. No existing site. No brand implementation. Just a tight deadline and high expectations.

**Features we added:**
- Live job portal connected to Sourceflow's talent acquisition software
- Blog for publishing recruitment insights and updates
- Lead capture forms for connecting headhunters with candidates
- Fully mobile-responsive design

**What we did for them:**
- Built and launched the landing page from scratch
- Integrated Sourceflow's job portal directly into the site
- Designed custom components aligned to the client's brand identity
- Connected lead forms to their recruitment pipeline

**Technologies used:** React, Tailwind CSS

## Case Study 2

- **Client name:** Aero Global Fuels Sdn Bhd
- **Industry:** Aerospace
- **What they do:** Aero Global Fuels supplies jet fuel to private, commercial, and government aircraft across Malaysia.
- **What we built:** A full website redesign — modernising their online presence and fixing a broken lead generation pipeline.

**The problem they had before us:**
Their website looked years out of date, key pages had broken links, and lead forms weren't delivering enquiries to their inbox. Potential clients were landing on the site and leaving with no way to get in touch.

**Features we added:**
- Working lead generation forms that actually reach the team
- Custom page designs that reflect the professionalism of their operation
- A clear display of credentials, fleet capabilities, and company portfolio

**What we did for them:**
- Redesigned and rebuilt the entire website
- Developed the site on WordPress for easy in-house management
- Implemented and tested all lead capture forms
- Showcased their credentials, services, and company portfolio
- Provided ongoing, on-demand website fixes post-launch

## Case Study 3

- **Client name:** AWAM
- **Industry:** Non-Profit / NGO
- **What they do:** AWAM is an independent feminist non-profit advancing women's rights in Malaysia — running advocacy campaigns, support services, and public education programmes.
- **What we built:** A refreshed website that gives AWAM a modern digital home to share their work, publish reports, and connect with supporters.

**The problem they had before us:**
Their existing site had an outdated design that no longer reflected the weight of their work. Broken links were frustrating visitors, and enquiry forms weren't delivering messages — meaning supporters and press contacts were falling through the cracks.

**Features we added:**
- Lead generation and contact forms with reliable delivery
- A blog to publish recent campaigns, news, and updates
- Annual reports section for transparency and stakeholder communications
- Custom page designs for each section of the organisation

**What we did for them:**
- Redesigned the full website from the ground up
- Developed the site on WordPress for the team to manage independently
- Fixed all broken links and contact form failures
- Presented their portfolio of work, credentials, and impact clearly
- Provided ongoing, on-demand support after launch
